Rules and Regulations-

  1. There are 6 questions to be attempted.
  2. There are three levels of questions :

    • Easy

      :

      These questions are worth 100 points.

    • Medium

      :

      These questions are worth 200 points.

    • Hard

      :

      These questions are worth 300 points.

  3. There is a bonus question.
    • It is divided into 3 parts - Easy, Medium, and Hard.
    • The 3 parts contain the same marking scheme as the regular questions, bringing the total marks up to 600 points.
    • For the bonus question only finding the bugs will suffice for full marks.
  4. In case you cannot solve the bug, simply add a comment to indicate where the bug is.
  5. Finding the bug will give you half of the available points per question. Solve the bug for full points.
  6. To attempt a question click on the "Attempt" button and download the zip file. Check the codes inside the zip files and successfully identify the bugs they contain.
  7. Solve as many questions possible within the specified time limit of 1 hour.
